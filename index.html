About Ozamiz
With a rich and storied past, this city has grown from a small Spanish settlement into a thriving hub of culture, commerce, and faith. Originally established as a defense outpost in the 18th century, it played a crucial role in protecting the region from pirate invasions. Over time, it evolved into a vibrant community, shaped by its historical landmarks, strong religious devotion, and resilient people. Renamed in honor of a national hero, it continues to preserve its heritage while embracing modern progress, making it a city that honors its past while looking toward the future. More About Ozamiz
